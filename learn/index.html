Diving into the fediverse

Fediverse is vast. It's made up of thousands of communities with millions of people like you, wanting to connect and share.

Here's a few resources that will help you get a better idea of how things work, how to find a community that's right for you, and explore tools you can use to make your experience even better.

  • joinfediverse.wiki: an encyclopedia dedicated to all things fediverse
  • fediverse.info: learn about fediverse, browse trending topics and communities, and more
  • fediverse.observer: find communities and dive deeper into the data about the fediverse
  • fediverse.party: a quick look into fediverse networks
  • fedi.tips: non-technical guide to using Mastodon and the wider fediverse
  • A netizen's guide to Mastodon and the fediverse
